Artificial Intelligence Law

Legal and regulatory impact assessment of AI on business operations, product development, and compliance under South Africa's evolving AI Policy Framework.

AI has become a significant introduction into business and impacts most sectors. The South Africa Department of Communications and Digital Technologies released an Artificial Intelligence Policy Framework in August 2024, which will form the basis of forthcoming legislation.

RSS Inc. provides advice on the legal and regulatory impact of AI on your business, including product development alignment with FICA, POPIA and Consumer Protection Law. Key regulatory issues include: prescribed AI governance, identification of AI-generated products, liability for AI-induced harms, protection of information and privacy, intellectual property ownership of AI, and the impact of AI on anti-money laundering controls.

Corporate Investigations
