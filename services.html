Artificial Intelligence Law

AI has become a significant innovation into business and impacts on most sectors. Like the internet, the introduction of AI into all sectors cannot be ignored. Failure to prepare your business or services to accommodate efficiencies and competitive advantage and respond to the risks of AI will have significant impact on business and services.

We advise on the legal and regulatory impact of AI on your business, product development, governance, compliance, and decision making. Our focus remains on practical implementation, ethical oversight, and risk mitigation while maintaining best business practice.

  • Preparation of AI governance and ethical use of AI.
  • Identification of AI-generated products.
  • Consideration of liabilities for the use of AI.
  • Intellectual property ownership of AI.
  • The impact of AI on privacy, labour, and consumer protection.

  • Drafting and reviewing agreements for products and services.

Pension and Employment law

Environmental and Sustainability Law

Focuses on legal protection for the environment and sustainable development practices. The shifting skill required by Africa has resulted in a key aspect of sustainability becoming a business strategy and determining what good sustainable practice your client can adopt sustainably aligns with effective and easy to implement and create value.

  • Legal and regulatory advice in relation to environmental law.

Data Protection and Privacy Legislation

Ensures privacy rights and personal handling of personal data in compliance with laws like GDPR. Information is the most valued commodity in most sectors and the Protection of Information legislation protects information dissemination and use.

  • Implementation into privacy regulatory frameworks with local legislation where applicable.
  • Advice on the collection and processing of personal information.
  • Creation of policies, manuals and awareness documents.
  • Compliance audits and practical remediation plans.

Regulatory and Compliance

Involves legal adherence to industry regulations, standards, and government requirements. Ensuring that organisations comply with legislative and other legal requirements is an essential aspect of the business, as non-adherence or non-compliance has a direct impact on the viability of the business and the liabilities and punitive measures that can accrue to the business, the Executive and the Board of Directors.

  • We draft submissions, conduct presentations and engagements to regulators, government departments and parliament on impact of regulation.
  • Interpretation of the law and policy frameworks and the delivery of practical business risk compliance advice.
  • Review regulatory frameworks applicable to target sectors and risk assessment.
🔍

Corporate Investigations

Focuses on legal investigations into corporate misconduct, including fraud and regulatory breaches. We investigate matters and provide practical recommendations that support remediation, governance improvement and accountability.

Legal professional privilege is central to the authenticity of communications between a client and its legal advisors. Any advice or communication between the attorney and client will fall into the ambit of privilege if it is legal advice and can only be waived by the person to whom the advice belongs.

Governance

Covers the structures, policies, and rules guiding corporate decision making and accountability. Effective governance frameworks ensure organisations have sufficiently documented and adequately implemented structures between management, the executive and the board.

  • Governance frameworks that align legal requirements and operational needs.
  • Advice on board oversight, delegated authority and reporting structures.
  • Development of policies and procedures for improved accountability.

Mining Law

Regulates legal aspects of mineral extraction, environmental impact, and community rights. Mining is one of the most important sectors in South Africa and our support covers licensing, operational compliance, sustainability and engagement with affected communities.

  • Drafting policies and standard operating procedures.
  • Support for stakeholder engagement and compliance management.
